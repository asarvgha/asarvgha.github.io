Biography

Ali Sarvghad is postdoctoral fellow at University of California, San Diego. His research interests include Visual Analytics, Information Visualization, and Healthcare Informatics. Currently, he is collaborating with Dr. Nadir Weibel and Dr. Davey Smith at UCSD to design and develop Avant-Garde, a multi-faceted visual analytics platform for HIV/AIDS data.

During his PhD, Ali worked under the supervision of Dr. Melanie Tory, at the University of Victoria, Canada. His research was focused on facilitating collaborative visual analytics by utilizing analysis history. He also designed and developed analytics software for wall and tabletop displays.
